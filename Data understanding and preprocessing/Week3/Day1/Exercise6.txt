
Columns Useful for Different Types of Analysis:

1. Avg hrs per day sleeping
---------------------------
Best for: Trend analysis, group comparison, distribution analysis
Why:

This is your main measure of sleep behavior.

You can compare it across age groups, sexes, and years.

You can also study whether Americans are sleeping more or less over time.

2. Sex
------
Best for: Group comparison
Why:

You can compare whether males, females, or “Both” groups sleep differently.

Useful for demographic insights.

3. Age Group
------------
Best for: Group comparison, demographic analysis
Why:

Different age groups may have different sleep patterns.

Allows you to answer questions like: “Do teens sleep more than adults?”
